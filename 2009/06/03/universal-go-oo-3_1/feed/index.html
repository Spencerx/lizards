]]> By: Petr Mladek https://lizards.opensuse.org/2009/06/03/universal-go-oo-3_1/#comment-1000 Thu, 04 Jun 2009 09:30:52 +0000 http://lizards.opensuse.org/?p=1206#comment-1000 We do our best to release Go-oo at the same time as the upstream OOo is released. Unfortunately, it is not that easy because Go-oo has many extra changes and they need to be stabilized as well. The Go-oo release is usually delayed by few days or even weeks. The OOo-3.1 release was quite complicated. The upstream release was delayed by more than one month. The Go-oo release was delayed even more because we decided to have there OpenXML export in a useful state. Also the extended row limit in Calc caused some regressions that were discovered too late. We will do our best to avoid such a big delay in the future.
